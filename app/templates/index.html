Connecting Chicago families

MatchMediation is committed to helping Chicago families with child custody by pairing them up with third party supervisors who assist in providing a positive environment for supervised visits.

Did you know?

  • Almost 50 percent of all marriages in the United States will end in divorce or separation.
  • 90% of parents settle child custody cases without a judge's ruling, while 4% of child custody cases require going to jail
  • Supervised visitation is aimed towards protecting the safety of children while also allowing parents to maintain contact with their children

How we help

MatchMediation helps families experiencing child custody conflicts connect and pair with third party supervisors who help supervise visitations between parents and their children in order to create a positive, healthy environment.
